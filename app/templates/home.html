Welcome to the ultimate school bell managing program, PyBell. ;-)

What's this?

It is a program capable of managing the school bell according to the bell times. For test purposes there is an option to play a selected sound directly. However, the biggest advantage of using PyBell is the power of remote access. You can upload new schedules (bell times) or sounds without having to touch the computer connected to the school radio system. Perhaps, sometimes you want to switch between preexisting schedules. It can do that too.

Source

The source code is available on GitHub. https://github.com/Zotyamester/PyBell
